Flash Card App

The flash card game has a GUI made with tkinter, displaying French words that flip to reveal their meaning after 3 seconds. The user indicates whether they know the meaning or not by clicking the "known" or "unknown" button. The French words that the user doesn't know are added to a list called "to learn", which the user can review at the end of the game. Overall, the mechanics of the game are simple and designed to help the user learn French vocabulary through repetition and reinforcement.
